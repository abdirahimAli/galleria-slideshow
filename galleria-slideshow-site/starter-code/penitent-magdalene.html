Penitent Magdalene is a painting by the Italian
artist Artemisia Gentileschi. It hangs in Seville
Cathedral. It has probably been in the cathedral
since the late 17th century. The painting's first
home was the collection of Fernando Enriquez
Afan de Ribera, from 1626 to 1637. She
returned to the subject later in the 1620s in
Mary Magdalene as Melancholy.
